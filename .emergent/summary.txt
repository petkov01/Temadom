- **Business Model:**
    - Companies can buy project contacts one by one (pay-per-lead at €25).
    - Alternatively, companies can subscribe for a monthly fee (€100) for unlimited access to all project contacts.

    - **Project Listings:** Clients post projects, companies browse them.
    - **Project Estimation:** When a client posts a project, the system should automatically provide a budget estimation based on the details provided.
    - **Company Profiles & Reputation:**
        - Profiles with portfolios (before/after photos).
        - A rating and review system where clients can leave feedback. Companies cannot delete or modify reviews.
    - **Services Information Page:** A dedicated page with detailed descriptions of each service, including the process and standard execution times.
